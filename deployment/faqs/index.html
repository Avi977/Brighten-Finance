Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our lending services

What types of loans do you offer?

We offer business loans for equipment, working capital, expansion, and SBA loans. We also provide car loans for new and used vehicles.

How fast can I get approved?

Most applications receive a decision within 24 hours. Once approved, funding can be available as soon as the next business day.

What are your interest rates?

Business loan rates start from 6.99% APR and car loan rates start from 3.99% APR. Your actual rate depends on your credit profile and loan terms.

What credit score do I need?

While we consider all credit profiles, generally a score of 650+ gives you the best rates and terms. We also consider other factors like income and business performance.

Are there any prepayment penalties?

No, there are no prepayment penalties on any of our loans. You can pay off your loan early without any additional fees.

How much can I borrow?

Business loans range from $5,000 to $5,000,000. Car loan amounts depend on the vehicle value and your creditworthiness.
